# 🛡️ Welcome to Panzerdroideka

My name is Willy Olsen. I am a dedicated Linux enthusiast focusing on system autonomy, 
immutability through the precise use of (chattr +i) and (Read-Only root), and extreme hardware optimization. 

Through "guerrilla tactics" in the terminal, I build systems that refuse 
to crash—whether it is to squeeze the very last drops of performance 
out of legacy hardware, or to tame modern PCs.

My philosophy is simple: 
The hardware is yours. 
The operating system should serve *you*, not the other way around. 
We must reuse more, understand more, and take back control.
